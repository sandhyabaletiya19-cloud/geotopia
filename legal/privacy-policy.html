🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Geotopia ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our geography learning platform.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, screen resolution
  • Cookies: To improve user experience and remember preferences
  • Voluntary Information: If you contact us, any information you provide

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve and optimize our platform
  • Understand how users interact with our content
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features and educational content
  • Respond to user inquiries

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • No collection of sensitive personal data
  • No selling of user data to third parties
  • Regular security reviews
🎓 Educational Purpose: Geotopia is an educational platform. We do not require user accounts or collect personal identifiable information for basic usage.

Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences (theme, language)
  • Analyze site traffic (anonymous analytics)
  • Improve user experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Fonts (for typography)
  • Font Awesome (for icons)
  • Leaflet Maps (for interactive maps)
  • Unsplash (for images)

These services have their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Geotopia is designed to be educational and safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
