Questions? Answered.

Monthly vs Yearly — what's the difference?

Same features, different billing. Yearly saves you a lot — up to 56% compared to monthly. Monthly is great if you want to try before committing to a full year.

What happens when my plan expires?

You automatically go back to the Basic (free) plan. You won't lose any data — just the premium features will lock again. You can renew anytime to get access back instantly.

Why is UPSC only available in India?

UPSC (Union Public Service Commission) is India's civil services exam. The content is specifically designed for Indian competitive exam aspirants. International users can access all other plans!

How do international payments work?

International users pay via PayPal in USD. Indian users pay via Razorpay (UPI, Cards, Net Banking, Wallets). Your region is detected from your account settings.
