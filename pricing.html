Questions? Answered.

Monthly vs Yearly — what's the difference?

Same features, different billing. Yearly saves you significantly compared to monthly. Monthly is great if you want to try before committing to a full year.

Why is UPSC only available in India?

UPSC (Union Public Service Commission) is India's civil services exam. The content is specifically designed for Indian competitive exam aspirants. International users get the full Pro plan experience!

Why is Ultimate India-only?

Ultimate includes UPSC content which is India-specific. International users get the Pro plan which has everything except UPSC — that's the best value for global users!

What happens when my plan expires?

You automatically go back to the Basic (free) plan. You won't lose any data — just the premium features will lock again. You can renew anytime to get access back instantly.

How do international payments work?

International users pay via PayPal in USD. Indian users pay via Razorpay (UPI, Cards, Net Banking, Wallets).

What's the Early Bird offer?

The first 11 users to purchase the Ultimate Yearly plan get ₹100 off — paying ₹2,399 instead of ₹2,499. Once all 11 spots are taken, the offer ends permanently.

Can I get a refund?

Yes! We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not happy for any reason, email us within 7 days and we'll refund you — no questions asked.
