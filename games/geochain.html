⛓️ The Chain Concept

In GeoChain, every answer leads to the next question. Your knowledge must flow seamlessly from one topic to another, creating an unbroken chain of geographic connections.

🔗 Chain Example

"Capital of France?"
→
Paris
"River flowing through Paris?"
→
Seine
"The Seine empties into?"
→
English Channel

📋 Rules

  • Answer before the timer runs out
  • One wrong answer breaks the chain
  • Longer chains earn higher scores
  • Speed bonuses reward quick answers
  • Complete the full chain for victory
