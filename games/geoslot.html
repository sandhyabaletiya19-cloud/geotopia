🌍 How GeoSlot Works

🎯 Educational Objective

GeoSlot transforms learning geography into an engaging game. Every spin teaches you something new about our world!

🎰 How to Play

  • Spin the reels to get a geography question
  • Answer correctly to earn Knowledge Points & Coins
  • Build streaks for bonus multipliers
  • Level up by mastering different categories

📚 Categories

  • Capitals: World capital cities
  • Countries: Nations & territories
  • Landmarks: Famous places
  • Geography: Rivers, mountains, oceans
  • Flags: National flags
  • Culture: Languages, currencies, customs

🏆 Rewards

  • +100 Knowledge Points per correct answer
  • Streak bonuses multiply your rewards
  • Level up rewards unlock new features
  • Master categories to earn achievements

📖 Study Mode

Enable Study Mode to see the correct answer before moving on, perfect for learning new topics!
