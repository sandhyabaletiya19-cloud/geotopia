How to Play 🎮

🎯 Goal

Find and click all cards in order: 1 → 2 → 3 → 4...

🃏 Rules

  • Click a card to see its number
  • Correct card = stays open!
  • Wrong card = ALL cards flip back
  • Remember where numbers are!

⭐ Stars

  • ⭐⭐⭐ = Complete in 1 try
  • ⭐⭐ = Complete in 2-3 tries
  • ⭐ = Complete in 4+ tries

🌍 Word Levels

  • After level 50, spell words!
  • Find letters in order: E-A-R-T-H
  • Geography words: countries, cities...
