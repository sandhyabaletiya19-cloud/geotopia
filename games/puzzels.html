How to Play

🎯 Objective

Solve geography word puzzles before time runs out! Each mode offers a unique challenge to test your knowledge.

🎮 Game Modes

  • Word Scramble: Rearrange jumbled letters to form country/capital names
  • Word Chain: Enter a geography word starting with the last letter shown
  • Geo Riddles: Solve clever riddles about places around the world
  • Missing Letters: Fill in the blanks to complete geography terms
  • Trivia Twist: Choose the correct answer from multiple options
  • Mini Crossword: Complete crossword puzzles with geography clues

⚡ Scoring System

  • Base points: 100 per correct answer
  • Speed bonus: Up to +50 for quick answers
  • Streak multiplier: x1 → x2 → x3 → x4
  • Hints cost: -50 points each
  • Perfect round bonus: +100

💡 Tips

  • Use hints wisely - they cost points!
  • Build streaks for higher multipliers
  • Speed matters for bonus points
  • Practice different modes to improve
