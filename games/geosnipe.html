How to Play

🎯 Objective

Click on the correct country/capital on the real world map before time runs out!

🎮 Game Modes

  • Flag Hunter: See a flag, click the country
  • Capital Strike: Find the capital city
  • Country Snipe: Locate the country by name

⚡ Scoring

  • Base points: 100
  • Speed bonus: Up to +50
  • Streak multiplier: x1 → x4
  • Closer clicks = bonus points!

⌨️ Controls

  • Space: Pause/Resume
  • Esc: Quit to Menu
  • Scroll: Zoom map
  • Drag: Pan map
