How to Play

🎯 Objective

Click the correct location on the real world map before time runs out!

🎮 Modes

  • Flag Hunter: See flag, click country
  • Capital Strike: Find the capital city
  • Country Snipe: Locate by name

⚡ Scoring

  • Base: 100 points per correct
  • Speed bonus: Up to +50
  • Streak multiplier: x1 → x4

📱 Controls

  • Tap/Click to select location
  • Pinch to zoom the map
  • Drag to pan the map
