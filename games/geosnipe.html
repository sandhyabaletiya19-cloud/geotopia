How to Play

🎯 Objective

Click the correct location on the map before time runs out!

🎮 Modes

  • Flag Hunter: See flag, click country
  • Capital Strike: Find the capital
  • Country Snipe: Locate by name

⚡ Scoring

  • Base: 100 points
  • Speed bonus: Up to +50
  • Streak: x1 → x4 multiplier
  • Accuracy bonus for close clicks!

📱 Controls

  • Tap/Click: Select location
  • Pinch: Zoom map
  • Drag: Pan map
