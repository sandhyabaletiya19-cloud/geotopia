How to Play GeoSnipe

🎯 Objective

Identify and click the correct country/capital on the map before time runs out. Build streaks for combo multipliers!

🎮 Game Modes

  • Flag Hunter: See a flag, click the country
  • Capital Strike: Find the capital city
  • Country Snipe: Locate the country by name

⚡ Scoring

  • Base points for correct answer: 100
  • Speed bonus: Up to +50 points
  • Streak multiplier: x1 → x2 → x3 → x4
  • Miss 3 times = Game Over

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

Space Pause/Resume
R Restart
M Toggle Sound
Esc Quit to Menu
