How to Play

🎯 Objective

You'll be shown a corrupted flag with wrong colors, missing symbols, or scrambled elements. Your mission is to restore the flag to its original glory using the forge palette!

🔨 How to Forge
  • Tap a stripe on the flag to select it
  • Choose the correct color from the palette
  • Add or change emblems/symbols as needed
  • Submit when you think it's correct!
