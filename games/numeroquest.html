How to Play

🎯 Objective

Find and click cards in sequential order. Cards show their value briefly when clicked, then flip back. Remember where each number/letter is!

🎮 Number Levels (1-50)

  • Click cards to find: 1 → 2 → 3 → ... → N
  • Correct card: Stays revealed, find next
  • Wrong card: ALL cards flip back, start from 1
  • Complete when all cards are revealed in order

🌍 Geography Levels (51+)

  • Cards contain letters of geography words
  • Spell the word in order: E → A → R → T → H
  • Words: Countries, Capitals, Landmarks, etc.
  • Same rules: wrong letter = restart!

⭐ Stars & Scoring

  • 3 Stars: Complete in 1 attempt
  • 2 Stars: 2-3 attempts
  • 1 Star: 4+ attempts
  • Fewer attempts = better memory!

💡 Tips

  • Click unknown cards first to memorize positions
  • Create mental patterns for card locations
  • Use the sequence display to track progress
  • Don't rush - accuracy beats speed!
