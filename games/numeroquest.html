How to Play

🎯 Objective

Find and tap cards in sequential order: 1 → 2 → 3 → ... until you complete all cards!

🎮 Gameplay

  • Cards are shuffled face-down
  • Tap a card to flip and reveal its number
  • Find the NEXT number in sequence
  • Wrong tap = Game Over (in Classic mode)

⭐ Scoring

  • Base Points: 100 per correct card
  • Combo Bonus: Streaks multiply points!
  • Speed Bonus: Faster = more points
  • Perfect Bonus: No mistakes = 3 stars!

🔧 Power-ups

  • 👁️ Peek: Briefly reveal all cards
  • 💡 Hint: Highlight the next card
  • 🛡️ Shield: Survive one mistake
  • ❄️ Freeze: Pause the timer

🔢 Number Systems

  • Decimal: 1, 2, 3, 4... (default)
  • Binary: 1, 10, 11, 100... (base 2)
  • Octal: 1, 2, 3...7, 10... (base 8)
  • Hexadecimal: 1...9, A, B, C... (base 16)
