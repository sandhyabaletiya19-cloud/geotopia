How to Play

🎯

Objective

Listen to audio clips and identify which country or region they belong to. Test your ears and global knowledge!

🎮

Game Modes

  • National Anthems: Recognize anthem melodies
  • City Ambiance: Identify cities by street sounds
  • Language Clips: Guess the spoken language
  • Nature & Wildlife: Match animal sounds to regions
  • Famous Landmarks: Iconic location sounds
  • Traditional Music: Folk instruments & melodies
⚡

Scoring

  • Base score: 100 points per correct answer
  • Speed bonus: Up to +50 extra points
  • Streak multiplier: x2, x3, up to x4!
  • First-try bonus: Extra 25 points
💡

Tips

  • Use headphones for the best experience
  • You can replay the sound multiple times
  • Pay attention to unique cultural elements
  • Build streaks for maximum points!
