📖 Detective Handbook

🎯 Your Mission

Track "The Shadow" - a criminal mastermind fleeing across the globe. Use intelligence clues to identify their current hiding location. The fewer clues needed, the higher your reward!

🔍 Investigation Process

  • First intelligence clue is free
  • Click locked clues to reveal more intel
  • Each revealed clue reduces potential reward
  • Type your answer when confident
  • Capture with fewer clues = legendary detective!
