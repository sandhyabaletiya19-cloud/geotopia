📖 Detective Handbook

🎯 Your Mission

Identify the mystery country using as few clues as possible. Each case presents 5 clues that progressively reveal the location.

🔍 How It Works

  • First clue is always free
  • Click locked clues to reveal them
  • Each revealed clue costs points
  • Type your answer anytime you're confident
  • Solve with fewer clues = higher score!

  • Capital cities are often key hints
  • Population sizes narrow options fast
  • Unique features (only country with...) are goldmines
