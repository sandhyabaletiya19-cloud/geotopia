❓ How To Play

🎯 Objective

Answer as many geography questions as possible in 60 seconds! The clock is always ticking, so think fast!

📊 Categories

  • Capitals: Name the capital cities
  • Flags: Identify country flags
  • Landmarks: Famous world landmarks
  • Mountains: Peaks and ranges
  • Rivers: Major waterways
  • Deserts: Arid regions
  • Oceans: Seas and bodies of water
  • Population: Country populations

⚡ Scoring

  • Base: 100 points per correct answer
  • Speed bonus: Answer faster for more points
  • Combo: Build streaks for multipliers (x2, x3, x4)
  • Wrong answer: Combo resets to x1

⌨️ Controls

  • Click/Tap: Select an answer
  • Keys 1-4 or A-D: Quick select
  • Space: Pause/Resume
