HOW TO PLAY

Master the Vanishing World

👁️

WATCH THE MAP

The map starts with all country labels visible. Study their positions carefully - you'll need to remember them!
💨

LABELS VANISH

Every few seconds, one label disappears from the map. The vanished country's name appears at the bottom of the screen.
🎯

CLICK FAST

Click where the label used to be before time runs out!
  • Faster clicks = more points
  • Build streaks for multipliers
  • Miss = lose a life
🏆

SURVIVE & SCORE

Keep playing until you run out of lives. Higher difficulties give more points but less time. Can you reach the top?
