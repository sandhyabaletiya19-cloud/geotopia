How to Play

🎯 Objective

Drag country pieces from the side panel and drop them onto their correct positions on the map. Complete the puzzle before time runs out!

🎮 Game Modes

  • Timer Mode: Race against the clock to finish
  • Zen Mode: No timer, relax and learn
  • Challenge: Limited hints, strict time limits

📱 Controls

  • Drag pieces from the left panel
  • Drop them on the correct map location
  • Pinch to zoom the map
  • Use hints if you're stuck

⭐ Scoring

  • Faster completion = more stars
  • Fewer hints = higher score
  • Perfect accuracy = bonus points
