🎯 Memory Hooks & Mnemonics

🎪 SIM SIM NIFE - Layer Composition
SIAL = SIlica + ALuminium (Continental Crust)
SIMA = SIlica + MAgnesium (Oceanic Crust)
NIFE = NIckel + FErrum/Iron (Core)
🥚 Boiled Egg Analogy
Shell = Crust (thin, brittle)
White = Mantle (thick, semi-solid)
Yolk = Core (dense center)
📍 Discontinuities: "Come My Real Good Love"
Conrad → Moho → Repetti → Gutenberg → Lehmann
🌊 Seismic Wave Trick
"Passes through Liquid, Stops at liquid"
P-waves can travel through liquid outer core, S-waves cannot!

📦 Discontinuities Explained

  • Conrad: Within Crust (SIAL-SIMA boundary)
  • Moho: Crust-Mantle boundary (35 km avg)
  • Repetti: Upper-Lower Mantle transition
  • Gutenberg: Mantle-Core (2900 km depth)
  • Lehmann: Outer-Inner Core (5100 km)

🌊 Seismic Waves Comparison

  • P-Wave: Primary, fastest, all mediums
  • S-Wave: Secondary, medium speed, solids only
  • L-Wave: Surface, slowest, most destructive

🎯 Shadow Zones

  • P-Wave Shadow: 103° to 142° (ring-shaped)
  • S-Wave Shadow: Beyond 103° (entire far side)
  • Cause: Liquid outer core blocks/bends waves

🎯 Memory Hooks & Mnemonics

🎯 7 Major Plates: "PINEAPPLE SAUCE ANtarctica EATS"

🍜 Crackers on Soup Analogy
Crackers = Lithospheric plates (float on top)
Soup = Asthenosphere (semi-molten layer)
Heat from below = Convection currents (drive movement)
🏔️ Convergent Boundary Types
OO (Oceanic-Oceanic) = Islands (Japan, Philippines)
OC (Oceanic-Continental) = Volcanic Mountains (Andes)
CC (Continental-Continental) = Fold Mountains (Himalayas)

📦 Plate Boundaries

  • Divergent: Plates move apart (← →)
  • Convergent: Plates collide (→ ←)
  • Transform: Plates slide past (↑ ↓)

🌋 Convergent Subtypes

  • O-O: Island arcs + Trenches
  • O-C: Volcanic mountains + Trenches
  • C-C: Fold mountains (NO volcanism)

🔬 Seafloor Spreading

  • Harry Hess (1962)
  • New crust at mid-oceanic ridges
  • Magnetic stripes prove spreading
  • Age increases away from ridge
