Our Cosmic Address

Earth in the Universe

Our home planet is the third from the Sun, a small blue marble in the vast cosmic ocean, yet the only known place in the universe where life flourishes.

The Sun

Our star, 93 million miles away, provides all the energy that makes life possible on Earth.

The Moon

Earth's only natural satellite, influencing our tides and stabilizing our planet's rotation.

Milky Way

Our home galaxy, containing over 100 billion stars, with Earth located in the Orion Arm.

Planet Earth

The third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. Earth formed approximately 4.5 billion years ago, and life appeared on its surface within the first billion years.

With 71% of its surface covered by water and seven distinct continents, our planet supports an incredible diversity of ecosystems, from the frozen poles to tropical rainforests.
