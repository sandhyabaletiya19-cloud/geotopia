❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Geotopia? ▼

Geotopia is a free geography learning platform where you can explore countries, continents, mountains, rivers, and more! We make geography fun and interactive.

Is Geotopia free to use? ▼

Yes! Geotopia is completely free. All our features including the World Atlas, Games, Encyclopedia, and UPSC materials are available at no cost.

How can I track my progress? ▼

Click the Dashboard button (3 lines) in the header to see your explored countries, quiz scores, XP points, and achievements!

Can I use Geotopia for UPSC preparation? ▼

Absolutely! We have a dedicated UPSC section with study materials, map practice, and mock tests specifically designed for civil service exams.
