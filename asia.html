🌟 GLOBAL IMPORTANCE

Asia is the world's economic powerhouse and demographic giant:

  • 🏠 60% of world's population lives here
  • 📈 Home to fastest-growing economies (China, India, Vietnam)
  • 🛍️ World's largest manufacturing and export center
  • 🛢️ Controls majority of global oil reserves
  • 💻 Leading in technology and innovation
  • 🚢 Critical maritime trade routes (Straits of Malacca, Hormuz)
