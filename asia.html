📋 Overview of Asia

📍 Location

Largest continent in Eastern & Northern Hemispheres. Bounded by Arctic, Pacific & Indian Oceans.

🌐 Significance

30% of Earth's land, 60% of world population. Home to highest peak & largest lake.

🏛️ Heritage

Birthplace of major religions & ancient civilizations. Known as "Cradle of Civilization."

📊 Quick Facts

Total Area 44,579,000 km²

Most Populous China (1.4 billion)

🏔️ Physical Features

Major Mountain Ranges

🏔️

Himalayas

World's highest range with Mt. Everest. 2,400 km across 5 countries.

⛰️

Karakoram

Home to K2. Largest glaciers outside poles.

🗻

Hindu Kush

800 km range. Highest: Tirich Mir (7,708m).

🏔️

Ural Mountains

Natural Europe-Asia boundary. 2,500 km long.

☀️ Climate of Asia

Asia experiences all climate types due to its vast size — from arctic in Siberia to tropical in Southeast Asia.

Climate Type Region Temperature Rainfall
Tropical Monsoon South Asia, SE Asia 25-35°C 1500-3000 mm
Hot Desert Arabian Peninsula 20-50°C < 250 mm
Mediterranean Turkey, Israel 10-30°C 500-900 mm
Subarctic Siberia -50 to 20°C 200-500 mm
