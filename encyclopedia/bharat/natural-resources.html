🏔️ Top Coal Belt

Jharia Coalfield in Jharkhand - one of the most important sources of metallurgical coal in India, with reserves exceeding 19.4 billion tonnes.

⚙️ Largest Iron Ore Mine

Bailadila in Chhattisgarh produces some of the finest quality iron ore (65-67% Fe content) exported worldwide.

☀️ Solar Giant

Bhadla Solar Park in Rajasthan - the world's largest solar park with 2,245 MW capacity across 14,000 acres.

💧 Hydro Leader

Tehri Dam in Uttarakhand - India's highest dam (260.5m) with 1,000 MW installed hydroelectric capacity.

🟡 Gold

💰
Historic Mine: Kolar Gold Fields (Karnataka) - Closed
Active Mine: Hutti Gold Mine (Karnataka)
Production: ~1,600 kg/year
Historical Significance:

Kolar Gold Fields was one of the deepest mines in the world (3,000m) and operated for over 120 years before closure in 2001.

🟢 Chromite

⚗️
Top State: Odisha (98%)
Major Belt: Sukinda Valley
Primary Use: Stainless Steel, Refractories
Global Importance:

Sukinda valley contains 97% of India's chromite reserves and is among the largest chromite producing regions globally.
