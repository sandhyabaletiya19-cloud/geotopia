💡 Historical Significance

Kolar Gold Fields: One of the world's deepest gold mines (3,000 meters), operational from 1880-2001. Produced over 800 tonnes of gold. Known as the "El Dorado of India". British-era mine that contributed significantly to Karnataka's economy.

Hutti Gold Mine: Established in 1947, operated by BGML (Bharat Gold Mines Limited). Produces approximately 1,500-1,700 kg of gold annually. Uses underground mining techniques with modern processing facilities.
