Overview

A Land Rich in Natural Wealth

India possesses one of the world's most diverse geological formations, resulting in an extraordinary variety of mineral deposits. From the ancient Precambrian rocks of the Peninsular Shield to the young sediments of the Indo-Gangetic plains, the subcontinent is a treasure house of metallic ores, industrial minerals, and energy resources that have sustained civilizations for millennia.
