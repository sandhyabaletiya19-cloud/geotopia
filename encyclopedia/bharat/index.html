The Siachen Glacier

At an altitude of 5,753m (18,875 ft), the Siachen Glacier in the eastern Karakoram range is the highest battlefield in the world. At 76 km long, it's the longest glacier in the Karakoram and second-longest in the world's non-polar areas. Indian troops have been stationed here since 1984 under Operation Meghdoot.

Location: Ladakh Length: 76 km Elevation: 5,753m Feeds: Nubra River
