International Borders of India

India shares its borders with 7 nations across 15,106.7 km of land boundaries and 7,516.6 km of coastline — one of the most complex geopolitical border networks in the world.

📌 Key Highlights

🇧🇩

Longest Border — Bangladesh

4,096 km across 5 Indian states. Enclave exchange completed in 2015.

🇨🇳

LAC with China

3,488 km Line of Actual Control. Most disputed & strategically sensitive.

🇵🇰

Radcliffe Line & LoC

3,323 km with Pakistan. Includes LoC in Kashmir & IB in plains.

🇳🇵

Open Border — Nepal

1,751 km open border. Citizens can cross freely without passport.

🛡️

BSF — Largest Border Force

Guards Pakistan & Bangladesh borders. ~2.65 lakh personnel strong.

🏔️

ITBP on China Border

Indo-Tibetan Border Police guards 3,488 km of Himalayan frontier.

🛡️ Border Management Forces

🟢 BSF

Border Security Force
India's primary border guarding force. Largest border security force in the world with ~2.65 lakh personnel. Responsible for maintaining vigil along the International Border (IB).
Pakistan & Bangladesh Borders

🔴 ITBP

Indo-Tibetan Border Police
Specialized mountain warfare and border guarding force. Deployed across high-altitude Himalayan terrain from Karakoram Pass (Ladakh) to Jachep La (Arunachal Pradesh).
China Border (LAC)

🟡 SSB

Sashastra Seema Bal
Deployed on open/friendly borders. Performs border guarding, civic action programs, and anti-smuggling operations in border areas.
Nepal & Bhutan Borders

🟣 Assam Rifles

Assam Rifles — "Sentinels of the Northeast"
India's oldest paramilitary force (est. 1835). Counter-insurgency operations and border guarding in the challenging hilly terrain of Northeast India.
Myanmar Border

🔵 Indian Coast Guard

Indian Coast Guard
Maritime border security, coastal surveillance, anti-smuggling, anti-piracy, and search & rescue. Works in coordination with the Indian Navy for maritime security.
Entire Coastline & EEZ

⚪ Indian Army

Indian Army — LoC & LAC Deployment
Directly manages the Line of Control (LoC) in Jammu & Kashmir and forward areas along the LAC. Conducts operations in Siachen Glacier — the world's highest battlefield.
LoC, LAC, Siachen
