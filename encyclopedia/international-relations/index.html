IR Through History

Milestones that shaped modern international relations

1648

Peace of Westphalia

Birth of the nation-state system. Sovereignty became the cornerstone of international law.

1815

Congress of Vienna

Post-Napoleonic order. Balance of power doctrine established in Europe.

1919

League of Nations

First attempt at global governance. Laid groundwork for the United Nations.

1945

United Nations Founded

Post-WWII global order. 193 member states. Security Council established.

1947–1991

Cold War Era

Bipolar world: USA vs USSR. Proxy wars, nuclear deterrence, space race.

1991–NOW

Multipolar World

Rise of China, EU expansion, BRICS, cyber warfare, and digital diplomacy.
