What Are International Relations?

🌐

The Study of Connection

International Relations examines how nations interact—through diplomacy, trade, conflict, cooperation, and culture. It's the story of humanity organizing itself across borders.

🎯

Why It Matters

Every global issue—climate change, pandemics, poverty, peace—requires countries to work together. Understanding these relationships is understanding our collective survival.
