The Grand Timeline of Earth

From the violent birth of our planet 4.5 billion years ago to the complex geopolitical landscape of today, Earth's geography has been in constant flux. Continents have drifted, oceans have risen and fallen, civilizations have emerged and vanished, empires have expanded and crumbled. This interactive journey reveals how the physical and political geography of our world has shaped—and been shaped by—the forces of nature and humanity.
