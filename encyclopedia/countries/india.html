Unique Geographic Features

🌟 Did You Know?

India is the only country named after a river (Indus/Sindhu). The country is a subcontinent - a landmass with distinct geographical identity!

🌟 Geographic Record

Mawsynram in Meghalaya receives the highest rainfall in the world (11,871 mm annually). Cherrapunji is 2nd!

🌟 Extreme Points

• Northernmost: Indira Col, Siachen Glacier
• Southernmost: Indira Point, Nicobar (submerged after 2004 tsunami)
• Easternmost: Kibithu, Arunachal Pradesh
• Westernmost: Ghuar Mota, Gujarat

Global Strategic Importance
Controls Indian Ocean; Bridge between East & West; Nuclear power; Major emerging economy
