⏱️ Water Residence Times

🌊 Oceans ~3,200 years Slowest turnover, deep ocean circulation
🧊 Glaciers 20-100 years (surface) to 1,000,000+ years (deep ice) Varies greatly by location
💧 Deep Groundwater 10,000+ years Fossil water, essentially non-renewable
💧 Shallow Groundwater 100-200 years Actively recharged aquifers
🏞️ Lakes ~17 years (average) Varies from days to centuries
🌱 Soil Moisture 1-2 months Rapid uptake by plants and evaporation
🏞️ Rivers ~2-6 weeks Continuous flow to oceans
☁️ Atmosphere ~9 days Fastest turnover in the cycle

🗺️ Global Drainage Basins

Atlantic Ocean Basin: Drains ~47% of global land area including Amazon, Congo, Mississippi, Niger, St. Lawrence, Rhine, Danube

Pacific Ocean Basin: Drains ~13% of global land area including Yangtze, Mekong, Columbia, Colorado, Murray-Darling

Indian Ocean Basin: Drains ~13% of global land area including Ganges, Indus, Zambezi, Irrawaddy, Tigris-Euphrates

Arctic Ocean Basin: Drains ~17% of global land area including Ob, Yenisei, Lena, Mackenzie

Endorheic (Inland) Basins: ~18% of global land area with no outlet to ocean including Caspian Sea, Aral Sea, Great Basin, Lake Chad
