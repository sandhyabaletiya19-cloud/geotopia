What is International Relations?

International Relations (IR) is the study of interactions between nations, encompassing diplomacy, trade, military alliances, cultural exchanges, and geopolitical strategies. It shapes the world order, influences global peace, and determines the economic prosperity of nations.

Why UNITY ATLAS?

This platform provides an unprecedented visualization of how 197 countries interact on the world stage. From ancient civilizations to modern superpowers, explore the complex web of alliances, conflicts, and partnerships that define our world.
