What is International Relations?

International Relations (IR) is the study of interactions among nations, including diplomacy, trade, conflict, cooperation, and the complex web of alliances that shape our world. It encompasses political, economic, military, cultural, and technological dimensions of how countries engage with one another.

Why UNITY ATLAS?

UNITY ATLAS exists to democratize geopolitical knowledge. By visualizing relationships between all 197 recognized nations, we empower citizens, researchers, and policymakers to understand global dynamics at a glance. Every nation matters. Every relationship counts.
