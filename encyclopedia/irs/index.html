What is International Relations?

International Relations (IR) is the study of interactions among nations, encompassing diplomacy, trade, conflict, cooperation, and the complex web of alliances that shape our world. It includes political, economic, military, cultural, and technological dimensions of how countries engage with one another on the global stage.

Why UNITY ATLAS?

UNITY ATLAS exists to democratize geopolitical knowledge. By visualizing relationships between all 197 recognized nations, we empower citizens, researchers, students, and policymakers to understand global dynamics at a glance. Every nation matters. Every relationship counts. Every voice deserves to be heard.
