📖 How Köppen Classification Works

First Letter: Main Group

  • A = Tropical (Tcoldest >18°C)
  • B = Dry (Evaporation > Precipitation)
  • C = Temperate (Tcoldest 0-18°C)
  • D = Continental (Tcoldest <0°C)
  • E = Polar (Twarmest <10°C)

Second Letter: Precipitation

  • W = Desert (very low precip)
  • S = Steppe/Semi-arid
  • f = No dry season
  • s = Dry summer
  • w = Dry winter
  • m = Monsoon
  • T = Tundra | F = Ice cap

Third Letter: Temperature

  • h = Hot (mean >18°C)
  • k = Cold (mean <18°C)
  • a = Hot summer (>22°C)
  • b = Warm summer (10-22°C)
  • c = Cool summer (1-3 mo >10°C)
  • d = Very cold winter (<-38°C)

📊 Climograph: Singapore

🔬 Why It Exists Here

  • 📍 Equatorial location (0-10° latitude)
  • ☀️ Constant solar radiation year-round
  • 🌀 ITCZ convergence = constant convective rain
  • 🌊 High sea surface temperatures nearby
  • 💨 Weak Coriolis effect prevents hurricanes

🌿 Defining Characteristics

  • 🌡️ Hot year-round (25-28°C average)
  • 🌧️ No dry season (>60mm every month)
  • 💧 2000-4000mm annual rainfall
  • ⛈️ Daily afternoon thunderstorms typical
  • 💦 High humidity (80-90% year-round)

🌳 Natural Environment

  • 🌲 Tropical rainforest biome
  • 🦜 Highest biodiversity on Earth
  • 🌿 Multi-layered canopy (emergent, canopy, understory)
  • 🐒 Primates, jaguars, tree frogs, parrots
  • 🍄 Rapid decomposition, thin soils
⚠️ Climate Change Projection 2100
  • 📈 +2-3°C temperature increase
  • 🌧️ More intense but less frequent rain
  • 🔥 Increased drought and fire risk
  • ⬆️ Potential shift to Aw (savanna)
