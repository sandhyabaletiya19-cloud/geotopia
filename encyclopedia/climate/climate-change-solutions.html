💡 Solutions Exist

Climate Solutions

We have the technology. Renewable energy, efficiency, natural solutions, and policy tools to solve this. Here's what works.

🔧 The Solution Wedges

No single solution solves climate change. We need many "wedges" working together, each contributing emission reductions.

⚡

Clean Energy

73% of emissions

☀️ Solar Power

  • Cost down 89% since 2010
  • Now cheapest energy in history
  • Can power 100% of needs

💨 Wind Power

  • Cost down 70% since 2010
  • Offshore wind expanding rapidly
  • UK: 40%+ electricity from wind

⚛️ Nuclear

  • Zero carbon operation
  • Reliable baseload power
  • Small modular reactors emerging
🚗

Transport

16% of emissions

🔋 Electric Vehicles

  • Battery costs down 90% since 2010
  • EVs now cheaper to run
  • Many countries banning ICE by 2035

🚄 Public Transit

  • 10x more efficient per passenger
  • High-speed rail replacing flights

🚲 Active Transport

  • Bike infrastructure growing
  • E-bikes extending range
  • Healthier + zero emissions
🌳

Nature-Based

30% of solution potential

🌲 Reforestation

  • Could absorb 10 Gt CO₂/year
  • Biodiversity co-benefits
  • Must be done right (native species)

🌾 Regenerative Agriculture

  • Soil carbon sequestration
  • No-till farming
  • Cover crops

🌊 Blue Carbon

  • Mangroves, seagrass, kelp
  • Store carbon 4x faster than forests
  • Coastal protection co-benefits
🏠

Buildings

6% direct + 12% indirect
  • Heat pumps: 3-4x more efficient than gas
  • Insulation: Reduce heating 50-80%
  • LED lighting: 90% less energy
  • Smart thermostats: 10-15% savings
  • Building codes: Net-zero new construction
🏭

Industry

21% of emissions
  • Green steel: Hydrogen instead of coal
  • Green cement: New chemistry, CCS
  • Efficiency: Process improvements
  • Circular economy: Reduce, reuse, recycle
  • Electrification: Where possible
🌬️

Carbon Removal

Needed for net-zero
  • Direct Air Capture: Pulls CO₂ from air
  • BECCS: Bioenergy + carbon capture
  • Enhanced weathering: Rocks absorb CO₂
  • Ocean alkalinity: Increase absorption
  • Biochar: Stable soil carbon

⚠️ Note: Can't replace emission cuts. Needed for residual emissions.

📜 Policy Tools

💰

Carbon Pricing

Make polluters pay. Carbon tax or cap-and-trade. ~70 jurisdictions now have it.

📋

Regulations

Emission standards, efficiency mandates, ICE phase-outs, building codes.

💵

Subsidies

Support clean tech: EVs, solar, heat pumps. Remove fossil fuel subsidies ($5.9T/year).

🔬

R&D Investment

Fund breakthrough technologies: storage, hydrogen, fusion, DAC.
