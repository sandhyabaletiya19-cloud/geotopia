🔥 Heat Phenomena

🌡️

Heat Wave

Prolonged extreme heat

Definition (varies by region)

  • 2+ days >90th percentile temps
  • Or >35°C for 3+ days
  • Relative to local climate

Deadliest Heat Waves

  • Europe 2003: 70,000+ deaths
  • Russia 2010: 55,000 deaths
  • India 2015: 2,500+ deaths
  • Pacific NW 2021: 49.6°C in Canada

💀

Wet Bulb Temperature

Survivability limit

The Fatal Threshold

35°C WBT
Human body cannot cool itself

What It Means

Wet bulb temp = temperature accounting for humidity. At 35°C WBT, sweating no longer works. Even healthy people in shade with unlimited water die within hours. Parts of Gulf and South Asia approaching this.

Examples

  • 40°C + 75% humidity = 35°C WBT (fatal)
  • 45°C + 50% humidity = 35°C WBT (fatal)
  • 50°C + 20% humidity = 30°C WBT (survivable)

❄️ Cold Phenomena

🥶

Cold Wave

Rapid temperature drop

Causes

  • Polar vortex breakdown: Arctic air spills south
  • Cold air outbreaks: High pressure systems
  • Radiational cooling: Clear nights

Wind Chill

Wind increases heat loss from exposed skin. At -30°C with 40 km/h wind, frostbite can occur in under 10 minutes.

🌀

Polar Vortex

Arctic circulation pattern

What It Is

Large area of cold air and low pressure around poles. When stable, keeps cold air in Arctic. When weakened (often by warming), allows cold air to spill into mid-latitudes.

2019 Event

Chicago reached -31°C (-52°C wind chill). Colder than Antarctica at the time. Schools, businesses closed for days.

💨 Famous Local Winds

🔥

Santa Ana

Southern California
  • Hot, dry winds from desert
  • Oct-Mar peak (fall fire season)
  • 60-100+ km/h gusts
  • Humidity drops below 10%
  • Prime wildfire conditions
  • "Devil winds" - linked to mood changes
🏔️

Chinook / Föhn

Rockies / Alps
  • Warm dry downslope wind
  • Temperature jumps 20-30°C in hours
  • 1943: Spearfish SD, +27°C in 2 minutes!
  • "Snow eater" - melts snow rapidly
  • Föhn headaches common in Alps
🌬️

Mistral

Southern France
  • Cold dry northerly wind
  • Funnels down Rhône Valley
  • 100+ km/h possible
  • Clears skies, good visibility
  • Can blow for days
  • Cypress trees bend permanently
🏜️

Sirocco

Mediterranean
  • Hot dusty wind from Sahara
  • Carries red desert dust
  • Can raise temps 20°C
  • Deposits dust across Europe
  • "Blood rain" when mixed with precipitation
❄️

Bora

Adriatic Sea
  • Cold dry katabatic wind
  • Descends from mountains to coast
  • 150+ km/h gusts possible
  • Creates dramatic sea spray
  • Can freeze instantly on contact
🌍

Harmattan

West Africa
  • Dry dusty wind from Sahara
  • Nov-Mar (dry season)
  • Reduces visibility severely
  • Drops humidity below 15%
  • Cools tropical coast temporarily

🌈 Atmospheric Optical Phenomena

🌈 Rainbow

Sunlight refracted and reflected inside water droplets. Always opposite the sun. Double rainbows have reversed colors. Full circle visible from aircraft.

🌌 Aurora

Solar wind particles excite atmospheric gases. Green from oxygen at 100-300km, red from oxygen above 300km, blue/purple from nitrogen. Best seen near magnetic poles.

☀️ Solar Halo

22° ring around sun from ice crystals in cirrus clouds. Often indicates incoming weather front. "Ring around the moon, rain by noon."

🔮 Sundogs

Bright spots 22° to left and right of sun. Caused by hexagonal ice crystals. Most visible when sun is low. Also called "mock suns."

🏜️ Mirage

Light bent by temperature gradient. Inferior mirage: hot surface creates "water" appearance. Superior mirage: cold surface creates inverted images (Fata Morgana).

💚 Green Flash

Brief green spot as sun sets/rises. Atmospheric dispersion separates colors, green last to disappear. Requires clear horizon (ocean view best).
