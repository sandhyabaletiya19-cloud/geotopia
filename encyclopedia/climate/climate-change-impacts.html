⚠️ Climate Tipping Points

Self-reinforcing changes that become irreversible once triggered. Some may already be crossed or close to it.

🧊 Greenland Ice Sheet

Threshold: ~1.5-3°C

Contains 7.4m of sea level rise. Once started, may be unstoppable. Already losing 280 billion tonnes/year. Full melt takes centuries but is becoming inevitable.

Near threshold

🧊 West Antarctic Ice Sheet

Threshold: ~1.5-2°C

Contains 3.3m of sea level rise. Marine ice sheet instability may be triggered. Some sectors already in irreversible decline. Could add meters to sea level in centuries.

May be triggered

🌳 Amazon Dieback

Threshold: 20-25% deforestation

Amazon creates 50% of its own rainfall. Past a threshold, it dries out and flips to savanna. Already at 17% deforested. Would release ~140 billion tonnes carbon.

Approaching

🧊 Permafrost Collapse

Threshold: ~1.5-2°C

Contains 1,500 billion tonnes carbon (2x atmosphere). Thawing releases CO₂ and methane. Already thawing faster than predicted. Could add 0.3°C warming.

Accelerating

🌊 AMOC Slowdown

Threshold: ~3-5°C

Atlantic circulation that keeps Europe warm. Already slowed 15%. Collapse would cool Europe 5-10°C, shift tropical rain belts, raise sea level on US East Coast.

Slowing

🌲 Boreal Forest Shift

Threshold: ~3-4°C

Taiga transitioning to temperate forest/grassland. Increased fires already releasing massive carbon. Albedo changes could accelerate Arctic warming.

Beginning

🗺️ Regional Impact Highlights

🌍 By Region

🌎 North America ▼
  • Western wildfires: +500% since 1970s
  • Hurricane intensity increasing
  • Southwest megadrought (worst in 1200 years)
  • Heat waves becoming deadly
  • Great Lakes water levels volatile
🌍 Europe ▼
  • Mediterranean: severe drought risk
  • Alps glaciers: 50% gone by 2050
  • Extreme heat: 2003, 2019, 2022 events
  • Flooding: Germany 2021 (180+ deaths)
  • UK: Climate like southern France by 2100
🌏 Asia ▼
  • Monsoon becoming more erratic
  • Himalayan glaciers: water for 2 billion
  • Bangladesh: 17% underwater at +1m sea level
  • Heat + humidity: wet bulb approaching limits
  • Typhoons intensifying
🌍 Africa ▼
  • Sahel: Drought + conflict nexus
  • Lake Chad: 90% shrunk since 1960s
  • Crop yields: -20% projected
  • Malaria expanding to highlands
  • Coastal cities: Lagos, Alexandria at risk
🌏 Pacific Islands ▼
  • Existential threat from sea level rise
  • Tuvalu, Kiribati, Marshall Islands at risk
  • Saltwater intrusion destroying crops
  • Coral bleaching destroying fisheries
  • First climate refugees already relocating
