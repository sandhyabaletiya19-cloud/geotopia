🌾 Agriculture

Farming Systems Worldwide

Understanding how the world feeds itself

🏡

Subsistence Farming

→ For family consumption

→ Small landholdings

→ Traditional methods

→ Low productivity

Examples: India, Africa, SE Asia
🚜

Commercial Farming

→ For sale/profit

→ Large scale operations

→ Mechanized farming

→ High productivity

Examples: USA, Australia, Canada
🌴

Plantation Agriculture

→ Single cash crop

→ Large estates

→ Export oriented

→ Labor intensive

Crops: Tea, Coffee, Rubber, Sugarcane
🐄

Mixed Farming

→ Crops + Livestock

→ Diversified income

→ Risk distribution

→ Sustainable practices

Examples: Europe, NE USA, New Zealand
