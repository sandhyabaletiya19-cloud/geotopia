🌍 Cultural Regions & Realms

🗺️ World Cultural Realms (De Blij Model)

🏛️

Western/European

  • North America, Europe, Australia, NZ
  • Democracy, individualism, capitalism
  • Christian heritage (now secular)
  • Scientific rationalism
  • Languages: English, French, German, Spanish
🕌

Islamic World

  • Middle East, North Africa, Central Asia
  • Sharia influence, communal values
  • Arabic as sacred language
  • Five Pillars framework
  • 57 OIC member countries
🛕

South Asian

  • India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Nepal, Sri Lanka
  • Hindu-Buddhist heritage
  • Caste system remnants
  • Joint family structure
  • Languages: Hindi, Bengali, Urdu, Tamil
🏯

East Asian

  • China, Japan, Korea, Taiwan
  • Confucian values
  • Education emphasis
  • Collectivism, hierarchy
  • Characters-based writing
🌍

Sub-Saharan African

  • 48 countries south of Sahara
  • Tribal/ethnic identities
  • Oral traditions
  • Communal land ownership
  • 2,000+ languages
💃

Latin American

  • Central & South America, Caribbean
  • Spanish/Portuguese colonial legacy
  • Catholicism dominant
  • Mestizo populations
  • Extended family values
🔄 Cultural Diffusion

📡 Types of Cultural Diffusion

✈️

Relocation Diffusion

People physically move, bringing culture

Migration, colonization
📊

Hierarchical Diffusion

Spreads from authority centers down

Fashion from Paris, tech from Silicon Valley
🌊

Contagious Diffusion

Spreads outward from source equally

Viral trends, language slang
📺

Stimulus Diffusion

Idea sparks local adaptation

McDonald's menu variations
