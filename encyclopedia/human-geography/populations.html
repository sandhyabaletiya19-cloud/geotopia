Regional Patterns

  • Eastern Europe: Declining populations • Outmigration • Low fertility trap

DTM Country Case Studies

Case Study: Japan (Stage 5)

  • Total Fertility Rate: 1.26 (well below replacement 2.1)
  • Population Decline: -0.53% annually • Projected 88M by 2065
  • Aging Crisis: 29.8% over 65 • Highest in world
  • Policy Response: Immigration relaxation • Childcare incentives • Automation investment

Case Study: Nigeria (Stage 2)

  • Total Fertility Rate: 5.3 (very high)
  • Population Growth: +2.5% annually • Doubling time 28 years
  • Youth Bulge: 43% under 15 • Median age 18.6
  • Challenges: Job creation • Education • Infrastructure strain

Case Study: India (Stage 3)

  • Total Fertility Rate: 2.0 (near replacement)
  • Population Growth: +0.7% annually • Peaked, now declining
  • Demographic Dividend: 68% working age (15-64) • Window until 2040
  • Regional Variation: Kerala (1.6 TFR) vs Bihar (3.0 TFR)
