The Epidemiological Transition
Countries progress through stages: Stage 1: Infectious diseases dominate (Africa) → Stage 2: Declining pandemics (SE Asia) → Stage 3: NCDs emerge (Latin America) → Stage 4: NCDs dominate (Europe, N. America) → Stage 5: Re-emergence of infections (emerging)
