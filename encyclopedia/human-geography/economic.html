📊 Key Economic Indicators Explained

  • GDP (Gross Domestic Product): Total value of goods/services produced • Primary measure of economic size
  • GNI (Gross National Income): GDP + net income from abroad • Better for comparing living standards
  • PPP (Purchasing Power Parity): Adjusts for cost of living • More accurate comparisons
  • Gini Coefficient: 0 = perfect equality, 1 = perfect inequality • Measures income distribution
  • FDI (Foreign Direct Investment): Cross-border investments • Indicator of economic openness
