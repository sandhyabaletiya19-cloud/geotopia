🌍 Antarctic Treaty System

Antarctica has NO permanent population and NO countries. It is governed by the Antarctic Treaty (1959), signed by 54 nations. The treaty designates Antarctica as a scientific preserve, establishes freedom of scientific investigation, and bans military activity.
