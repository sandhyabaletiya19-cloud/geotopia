🌟 Key Facts About Asia

Essential information about the continent

🌏

Largest Continent

Asia covers 30% of Earth's land area and is home to 60% of the world's population

🏔️

Extreme Geography

Contains both the highest (Mt. Everest) and lowest (Dead Sea) points on Earth's surface

📜

Cradle of Civilization

Birthplace of writing, major religions, mathematics, and earliest civilizations

🏭

Manufacturing Hub

Produces over 50% of the world's goods, led by China, Japan, and South Korea

🍜

Culinary Diversity

Home to world's most diverse cuisines: Chinese, Indian, Japanese, Thai, Vietnamese, and more

💻

Tech Powerhouse

Leading in technology with companies like Samsung, Sony, Huawei, and thriving startup ecosystems
