🌟 Key Facts About Africa

Essential information about the continent

🦣

Cradle of Humanity

Africa is where modern humans (Homo sapiens) first evolved approximately 300,000 years ago

🏛️

Ancient Civilizations

Home to ancient Egypt, one of the world's first great civilizations dating back 5,000 years

🦁

Wildlife Paradise

Contains the world's greatest wildlife migrations and iconic species like lions, elephants, and gorillas

💎

Resource Rich

Contains 30% of Earth's mineral resources including gold, diamonds, oil, and coltan

🌍

Equator Crossing

The equator passes through 6 African countries: Gabon, Congo, DRC, Uganda, Kenya, and Somalia

📱

Tech Revolution

Mobile money (M-Pesa) was invented in Kenya and revolutionized banking across the continent
